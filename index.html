About Me

Hi, my name is Nav Dhillon! As technology becomes integral to daily life, I aspire to be at the forefront of designing and building innovative digital experiences and interfaces that blend functionality with striking visuals. My background in art and design gives me a unique multidisciplinary approach to programming, allowing me to bridge aesthetic vision and technical execution.

My technical foundation in Object-Oriented Software Development, studied at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, has allowed me to have significant experience in C# and the .NET Framework, with a focus on Windows desktop applications (e.g., WinForms). I excel at utilizing SQL and tools like SQL Server Management Studio, Visual Studio, and phpMyAdmin for efficient database creation, management, and integration.

My capabilities also extend to front-end web development, where I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create interactive, responsive, and highly efficient web applications. I am proficient in design tools like Figma and Canva for drafting and visualizing user experiences, and I'm actively broadening my understanding of UI/UX design principles and best practices.

Beyond core development, my academic background includes proficiency in Python for data manipulation alongside experience with CAD software like Autodesk Inventor for 3D modeling and technical drafting. This broad technical aptitude, coupled with my artistic foundation, allows me to approach problem-solving with a comprehensive and well-rounded methodology, as I continuously seek to learn and evolve in the programming landscape. I am commited to expanding my skillset through constantly exploring immerging technologies and conducting research on trends in the tech industry.
